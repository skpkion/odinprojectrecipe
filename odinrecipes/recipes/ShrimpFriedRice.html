Shrimp Fried Rice

(Even more Americanized)

Description

A shrimp fried this rice. (Serves 1+)


Flexible recipe, can be adjusted for more people. Everyone has a different taste preference, so ingredients are not exact.
Can substitute green onion for white onion (Recommend 1/2 onion for 4 servings)
Can also add eggs earlier in the recipe if you want.

Try not to use too much sesame oil (unless you like it that way).

Ingredients

  • Shrimp (As much as you want) (cooked or uncooked)
  • Eggs (As much as you want)
  • Rice (As much as you want)
  • Green Onions (As much as you want.)
  • Soy sauce to taste.
  • Sesame oil to taste. Use a lot less sesame oil than soy sauce.
  • Salt or pepper to taste.

Steps

  1. Cook rice in a pot/rice cooker on the side.
  2. When rice is almost done, cook shrimp in a pan/wok. If using white onion, cook it with the shrimp.
  3. Add rice to the pan.
  4. Season to taste.
  5. Add eggs.
  6. If using green onion, add it now.
  7. When eggs are done, take off heat and serve.
