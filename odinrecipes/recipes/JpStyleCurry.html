Japanese Style Curry

Description

Curry, in the style of the ones seen in Japan. Golden Curry Brand.

5 Servings

Ingredients

  • 2 Chicken thighs
  • 2 Potatoes
  • 3 Carrots
  • 1/2 Onion
  • Rice (As much as you want)
  • Golden Curry brand curry bricks
  • Lots of water
  • Garlic Powder (Optional)

Steps

  1. Cut chicken, carrots, and potatoes into bite size pieces. Dice onions.
    You may want to make the potatoes bigger than the carrots because they will melt into the curry and become very small.
  2. Cook rice in a pot/rice cooker on the side.
  3. Add oil to another pot (Med heat). Once hot, add onions and carrots and sweat them (1-2min).
  4. Add chicken to the pot. Fry until fond develops on the bottom.
  5. Add potatoes to the pot, and then add water to cover the potatoes.
  6. If using garlic powder, add it now.
  7. Bring to a boil, and then simmer till veggies are cooked.
  8. Keep on low heat and add curry cubes.
