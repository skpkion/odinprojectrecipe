Strawberry Banana Smoothie

Description

Strawberry Banana Smoothie.

If you are freezing your own fruit, it is recommended to freeze ripe banana. Clean and cut up the fruit prior to freezing, and freeze the fruit hours or days in advance.

You are free to include/exclude any ingredients on the list. You are also free to adjust the amount of each ingredient that you use. Any ingredient on this list can also be substituted.

Ingredients

  • 2 Cups frozen strawberries
  • 1 Frozen banana
  • 1/2 cup Milk
  • 1/2 cup Plain Yogurt (optional)
  • Honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Add frozen fruit and milk to a blender. Add anything else you want to the blender.
  2. Blend.
